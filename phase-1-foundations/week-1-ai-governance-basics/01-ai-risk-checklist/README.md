# Project 01: AI Risk Checklist for Datacenter Predictive Maintenance

## Objective

The objective of this project is to create a practical AI risk checklist for a hypothetical datacenter predictive maintenance AI system.

## Business Context

A datacenter may use AI to predict equipment failure, cooling issues, power anomalies, or maintenance needs.  
If this AI system fails, it can create financial loss, service downtime, safety issues, or compliance problems.

## Governance Focus

This project covers:

- AI risk identification
- Likelihood and impact scoring
- Risk prioritization
- Mitigation planning
- Enterprise AI governance thinking

## Files

| Folder | Purpose |
|---|---|
| notebooks/ | Google Colab notebook |
| data/ | Risk register CSV |
| reports/ | Written risk audit summary |
| outputs/ | Charts and visual outputs |

## Interview Explanation

In an interview, I can explain how I identified AI risks, scored them using likelihood and impact, prioritized them, and proposed mitigations from an enterprise governance perspective.
