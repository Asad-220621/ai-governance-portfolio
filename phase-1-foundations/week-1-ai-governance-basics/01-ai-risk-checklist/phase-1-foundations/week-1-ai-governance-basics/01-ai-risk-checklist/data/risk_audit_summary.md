
# AI Risk Audit Summary

## Project

AI Risk Checklist for Datacenter Predictive Maintenance

## Date

2026-05-04

## Objective

This report summarizes the key AI risks for a hypothetical datacenter predictive maintenance AI system.

## Method

Each risk was scored using:

Risk Score = Likelihood x Impact

Likelihood and impact were rated from 1 to 5.

## Highest Priority Risk

The highest priority risk is:

- Risk ID: R1
- Risk Type: Data Quality
- Risk Score: 20
- Risk Level: Critical

## Governance Interpretation

The risk register shows that enterprise AI systems require governance across data quality, model performance, cybersecurity, compliance, explainability, operations, monitoring, and ownership.

## Recommended Governance Actions

1. Define clear AI ownership and accountability.
2. Establish model monitoring and drift detection.
3. Keep human approval for critical operational decisions.
4. Perform periodic AI risk reviews.
5. Maintain audit logs and compliance documentation.
6. Create a fallback process if the AI system fails.
